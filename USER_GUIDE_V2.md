## What Is FoodMatch?

FoodMatch is a conversational restaurant discovery app for Valencia.

You ask in plain language: "Where can I eat good paella tonight, somewhere quiet, around 25 euros." You get three real recommendations with photos, current hours, and the menu. In Spanish or English. Including the small places Google does not surface.

Free for users. Forever. No subscription, no ads, no catch.

We make money by charging restaurants to manage their listings, not by charging you to find them.

### What makes us different

**Complete coverage of Valencia.** When we launch, every restaurant in the city will be in our index. Not the 1 to 2 percent that pay TheFork. Not the popular ones Google ranks. Every place you can eat.

**AI that actually understands you.** Type or speak in Spanish or English. Ask the way you would ask a friend who lives here. The AI gives you decisions, not lists.

**Real, current information.** Hours updated by the restaurant. Menus translated to your language. Photos that look like the actual food.

**Built for tourists and locals, side by side.** A British tourist on a 4-day trip and a local Valenciano student get equally good answers. Different needs, same quality.

## How FoodMatch Works

### Meet Laura

It's Saturday, 2pm. Laura just finished at the gym in Valencia. She's hungry, but doesn't know what she wants.

**Step 1: Open FoodMatch**

The home screen shows her nearest places: photo, rating, price range, distance. Better than scrolling Google Maps.

**Step 2: Ask the AI**

Laura taps the AI button and speaks: "Something light and healthy, walking distance, not too expensive."

The AI responds in seconds:

> "Three good options near you:
> - Green Bowl (200m): Poke bowls, around 15 euros, open until 4pm. Rating 4.6.
> - Fresh & Co (350m): Organic salads, around 12 euros. Rating 4.4.
> - Ramen House (400m): Light ramen, around 18 euros. Rating 4.7."

**Step 3: Check the Profile**

Laura taps Green Bowl. She sees real photos of the bowls, the menu with prices, recent reviews from other users, hours, distance, and a link to Google Maps.

**Step 4: Walk Over**

She taps "Get Directions," Google Maps opens, she walks the 200 meters.

**Step 5 (optional): Leave a Review**

After eating, FoodMatch asks how it was. She drops a quick rating and uploads a photo of her bowl.

Total time from hungry to walking out the door: about 2 to 3 minutes. Faster than calling a friend, more useful than a Google search.

## Why You'll Love FoodMatch

### AI That Actually Understands You

Talk to it like a friend who lives in Valencia:

- "I want a burger but make it fancy"
- "Somewhere romantic with a terrace"
- "Late night tacos, cheap and good"
- "Where can I get vegan brunch in Ruzafa, open Sunday morning"

The AI is grounded in our actual database. It does not invent restaurants. If we recommend a place, it exists, it's open at the hour we say, and the menu we show is real.

### Real Restaurants, Updated by Real Owners

The restaurants on our paid plan keep their own profile fresh. Today's menu, this week's specials, current hours during festivals (Fallas, San Juan), the new chef's photo. Information that other apps let go stale.

For free-listed restaurants, we pull from public data and tag them as "verify before going." Honesty over polish.

### Multilingual Without the Bad Translations

Tourist apps usually rely on auto-translation that turns "pulpo a la gallega" into "octopus from Galicia style" and confuses everyone. We use proper translations for menus, with the original Spanish kept underneath. You see what locals see, in a language you can read.

Spanish and English from day one. German, French, and Dutch added when we have demand.

### Free for You. Forever.

No premium tier. No paywalls on AI features. No ads selling you things you didn't ask for. The app costs nothing to use, fully.

We pay our bills by helping restaurants get found. Restaurants who join Pro pay €99 a month. Restaurants on the free tier are still in your search results, just with less rich profiles. Everyone is in. Quality of recommendation is the same.

## What We Are Not Promising

We want to be honest about what FoodMatch is and is not at launch. Some things you may have seen us discuss are great ideas but not in version one.

**Not in the launch version (we may build later if users love the core):**

- Friend activity feeds and social network features
- Live event and sports tracking ("which bar is showing the match?")
- Real-time food truck location tracking
- Badges, leaderboards, and ranks
- A credit reward system
- Reservations and ordering through the app
- Madrid, Barcelona, or other cities

We are starting with one job done extraordinarily well: helping a hungry person in Valencia find a great place to eat in under two minutes. Once that works for thousands of users and hundreds of restaurants, we earn the right to add the social and event features people will ask for.

If we tried to ship all of those at once, we would ship nothing well. Two founders, one focused product, then we grow.
