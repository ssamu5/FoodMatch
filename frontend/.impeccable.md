# Design Context - FoodMatch app

## Target audience
- Diners in Valencia (locals and visitors), Spanish-first with an English toggle, looking for where to eat tonight.
- Restaurateurs of small independent places who claim and manage their own listing.
- A tiny admin team (the two founders) reviewing claims.

## Use cases
- Natural-language restaurant discovery by craving, area, budget, vibe; a short, well-chosen shortlist (not 200 results).
- Save favourites and set a taste profile (synced per user once signed in).
- Restaurateurs claim a listing and edit its content.
- Admins approve/reject incoming claims.

## Brand personality / tone
- Warm, calm, food-forward, premium but friendly, distinctly Valencian. Not corporate, not flashy, not "techy".
- Design language (already in tailwind.config.js + src/index.css): "Uber x ChatGPT" calm. Cream page (`paper`), cards/inputs on a lifted WARM off-white `surface` (never stark white or pure gray/black). Elevation from soft diffuse shadows + hairline borders, not brutalist hard offsets. `tomate` (#e63946) is the single sharp accent; `mostaza`, `oliva`, `fresco`, `azulejo` are secondary.
- Type: Fraunces (display serif) + Bricolage Grotesque (body). Bilingual ES (default) / EN.
- Full light + dark mode via CSS variables.

## Quality bar
- Pre-launch product about to be submitted to the App Store / Play Store. Aim high (flagship feel) but pragmatic: consistency, polished states, and smooth micro-interactions over novelty. Do not redesign the established system; refine within it.

## Hard rules
- No em dashes or en dashes anywhere.
- Use the existing design tokens (paper/surface/tinta/tomate/etc.), never hard-coded colors or stark white/pure black.
