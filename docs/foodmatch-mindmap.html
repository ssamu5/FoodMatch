1
Core value

Core Value Proposition

Tell us what you are craving and we find the best version of that dish nearby, fast, with evidence.

  • Dish-first, not restaurant-first.
  • Beats endless scrolling.
  • Beats untrustworthy reviews.
2
Who

Customer Segments

  • Urban foodies.
  • Tourists and visitors.
  • People with allergy or dietary needs.
  • Restaurants with one standout dish and low visibility.
3
Why it matters

Jobs, Pains, Gains

Jobs
  • Find a craving fast.
  • Find hidden gems.
  • Decide without 20 to 30 min scrolling.
Pains
  • Regret of a mediocre place.
  • Restaurant-based, not dish-based, search.
  • Untrustworthy reviews (Google, TikTok).
  • Decision fatigue.
Gains
  • Best version of the craving.
  • Hidden gems.
  • Fast, confident decision.
4
What we build

Product and Features

  • Describe craving in plain text.
  • Dish and craving-level ranking.
  • Why recommended with evidence.
  • Taste personalization that learns.
  • Allergy awareness.
  • Discover hidden gems beyond influencers.
5
Build first

MVP Scope: 3 Core Features

  • 1. Craving input.
  • 2. 3 dish-level recommendations with short evidence.
  • 3. Feedback: good, bad, tried it, saved.
Plus one commit signal
Save WhatsApp Directions Restaurant lead

Lean-MVP rule: build the 20% that tests the core hypothesis, cut the rest.

6
Validate

MVP Validation Funnel

Problem-Solution Fit to MVP to Product-Market Fit
Continue criteria
  • 30 to 50 real Valencia testers.
  • 40% or more submit more than one craving.
  • 30% or more save or click a recommendation.
  • 10 or more users found a place faster than Google or TikTok.
  • 3 or more restaurants interested in paying.
Kill criteria
  • Users still prefer TikTok or Google.
  • Dish-level accuracy too hard.
  • No restaurant demand for dish-level leads.
7
Make money

Revenue Streams

Restaurant plans
  • Free public listing (built from public data) then claim or upgrade.
  • Pro 99 EUR/month (founder cohort 49 EUR/month for first 50).
  • Boost add-on 50 EUR/week (Pro only). Labelled "Promocionado", max 3 active per postal code, max 2 per AI response.
Future streams (BMC)
  • High-intent leads.
  • Commission on bookings or delivery.
  • Access to anonymised taste insights.
  • Premium guides.

0% commission is the current promise. Commission is a future, optional stream.

8
Supply side

Listing Lifecycle

PUBLIC_BASIC to CLAIM_PENDING to CLAIMED to VERIFIED
  • PUBLIC_BASIC: public data, looks populated day one.
  • CLAIM_PENDING: owner requests the listing.
  • CLAIMED: free, owner verified.
  • VERIFIED: paid plan, badge.

Fairness: public data helps diners discover. Restaurants can claim, correct, or remove their listing.
